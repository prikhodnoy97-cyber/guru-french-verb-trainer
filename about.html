About Guru French Verb Trainer

Guru French Verb Trainer is an interactive learning platform designed to help students practice and master French verb conjugation.

The platform includes:

  • Present tense
  • Future Simple
  • Passé Composé
  • Study Mode and Random Mode
  • Score and level system

Our mission is to make French learning simple, interactive and effective.
